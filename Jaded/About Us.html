About Us

Rinae (Trevor) Clothing is a South African streetwear brand which has its roots in the underground and
rebellious youth street culture that dominates the urban centres of Mzanzi. The Hoodie Corner Store is the
brainchild of Richfield group who are the founder and head designer of the brand. In 2024,
they saw a gap in the market for a brand that was designed by the youth for the youth, in
particular Generation Z. Through ambition, perseverance and
Team Team Ghost was born.


Rinae Clothing aesthetic is characterized by its innovative designs brought to life through a
21st-century African design philosophy that showcases the modern aspirations of South
African youth. The brand draws its inspiration from the “new age” youth of South Africa
who constantly redefines what it means to be African, young, wild and free.






Our Vision

Rinae Clothing aims to become the leading streetwear brand on the African continent. Inspiring
the youth through creative design, innovation and affordable pricing driven by the brand’s
mantra of“SMART • AFRICAN • AMBITIOUS”.
